## About

I am **Luis Angel Huanca Mamani**, a Systems Engineering student and software developer focused on building scalable digital products with a strong engineering mindset. My work combines **full stack development**, **mobile applications**, **cloud computing**, and **applied artificial intelligence** to create solutions that are useful, maintainable, and aligned with real user needs.

I have hands-on experience developing web and mobile applications using technologies such as **Vue.js, React, JavaScript, Flutter, Spring Boot, Python, and Java**, with a growing focus on cloud-native architectures and AI-powered systems. My background includes academic projects, hackathons, community initiatives, and professional experience in mobile development and document management optimization.

My engineering approach is based on clean architecture, continuous learning, teamwork, product thinking, and the ability to transform ideas into functional MVPs. I am especially interested in the intersection of **AI, cloud, software engineering, and social impact**, where technology can improve accessibility, productivity, and real-world decision-making.
